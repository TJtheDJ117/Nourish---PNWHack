Our Story:

At Nourish, our journey began with a shared passion for addressing two pressing issues: food waste and food insecurity. Witnessing the abundance of surplus food in restaurants combined with the prevalence of hunger within our local communities, we recognized an opportunity to create positive changeâ€“ both environmental and social. With a vision to bridge this gap and nourish both people and the planet, we set out to build a platform that connects restaurants with local community food services and shelters.

Through our innovative website, restaurants can easily donate their surplus food, and we ensure that it reaches the right hands in a timely manner. By partnering with local community food services and shelters, we forge strong relationships within the community and work together towards a shared goal of nourishing those who are most vulnerable.

Every day, we witness the transformative impact of our work as we see restaurants embrace sustainability practices, community food services receive nourishing meals, and individuals regain a sense of hope and security. Together with our partners and supporters, we are creating a world where no one goes to bed hungry, and where every plate of food contributes to a brighter, more sustainable future.

Join us on this incredible journey as we empower businesses, uplift communities, and nourish lives. Together, we can make a meaningful difference, one meal at a time.
