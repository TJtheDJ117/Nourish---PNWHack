Why Support nourish?

    The restaurant industry sheds $162 billion annually in food waste costs
    42 million Americans are coping with food insecurity
    Approximately 1/3 of the world's food production -- equivalent to around 1.3 billion metric tons -- is wasted annually. This waste includes edible food and also valuable resources like water, energy, and land.
    Wasted food aggravates water usage and deforestation, further causing an impact on the environment.
    Addressing food waste is crucial for a sustainable future, as it reduces greenhouse gas emissions and helps preserve valuable resources.
    Tackling food waste can contribute to mitigating climate change, conserving water, and promoting more efficient land use.
